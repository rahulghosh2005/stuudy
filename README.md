# Stuudy

**Strava for students.** A social study-tracking app where your live study timer broadcasts to followers and classmates in real time — making studying social, visible, and motivating.

## What It Does

- Start a study timer → your followers see you studying **live**
- Follow friends and classmates, see who's grinding right now
- Join **classrooms** (cohort groups) to hold each other accountable
- Track study history: streaks, course heatmaps, weekly summaries
- Log grades and GPA with public/private visibility controls
- Clean, premium UI — Notion-inspired layout, Apple-style animations, Strava-style progress visuals

## The Core Loop

> The moment you hit start, your followers know you're studying. That social pressure + motivation is the whole product.

## Stack

- **Next.js 14** (App Router, SSR + real-time)
- **Supabase** (auth, Postgres, real-time subscriptions)
- **Tailwind CSS** + **Framer Motion**
- **shadcn/ui** (heavily customized)

## Target Users

University/college students (18–25), studying in cohorts — same course, same program. The app only works if your friends are on it, so virality through classrooms is core.
